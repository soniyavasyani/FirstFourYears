About our School

First Four Years Preschool Literacy is a unique learning environment for children aged 2-6 years. Our Logo describes the different learning modules of our school. Like Mobile shows Digital Learning through tablets. To make sure that the thinking capability of a child is enriched, we do make the child Imagine by practicing Tactile Learning, i.e. TOUCH-FEEL-THINK. Then there is an Auditory Learning program where children come together in small groups with teachers to have fun, and learn to recognize the sounds of the alphabet and the links between reading and spelling. Finally the classic learning style, The Hand Hold Learning sums up with sand trays, tracing books, magic books, notebooks, slates, and many more... These methods guide and inspire us to maintain a high level of respect for each individual child, for the pursuit of knowledge. We view each child as a precious gift entrusted into our care and this belief translates into every day and each encounter at First Four Years Pre-School.
